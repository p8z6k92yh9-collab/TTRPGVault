### `=this.BookName`  

**_Karum Station Blues_** is an introductory scenario for **_Across a Thousand Dead Worlds_**, designed to immerse players in a gritty, high-stakes sci-fi universe. Starting off on the remote and perilous Karum Station, the scenario guides players through the station's dark underbelly, serving as the perfect introduction to the game’s setting, and gives players the option to undertake their first mission: a journey to the treacherous depths of a mysterious alien site known as Leviathan’s Chandelier. The book provides a richly detailed setting, complete with unique locations, dangerous events, and a host of alien threats. With its blend of atmospheric storytelling, tactical gameplay, and cosmic horror, **_Karum Station Blues_** offers a gripping introduction to the **_Across a Thousand Dead Worlds_** system.

  

**Key Features**:

- **Immersive Setting**: Explore the gritty, high-tech world of Karum Station, a hub of danger and intrigue on the edge of known space.
    
- **New Activities and Locations for Karum Station**: Whether you want to check out the Scab Markets, or purchase a casket to ensure your survival in case your next mission goes awry, there are plenty of things to do aboard Karum!
    
- **Leviathan’s Chandelier**: Dive into a unique alien site filled with environmental hazards, mysterious artifacts, and deadly creatures.
    
- **Rich Narrative**: Experience a story-driven adventure with multiple outcomes, influenced by player choices and actions.
    
- **Customizable Experience**: As usual, play with or without a Game Master, using the game’s flexible protocols to suit your group’s style.
    
- **Expanded Bestiary**: Encounter a variety of alien creatures, each with unique abilities and behaviors, adding depth and challenge to the gameplay.
    
- **Replayability**: With randomized events, multiple mission outcomes, and the option for repeated visits to the Chandelier, the scenario offers high replay value.
